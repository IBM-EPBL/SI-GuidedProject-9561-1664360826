Do's & Dont's

DO'S

Stay hydrated, Drink plenty of liquids,, Keep pressure on the dressing for about 3 minutes and the plaster for 6 hours, Wear comfortable clothes, Don't forget to share your medical history, Take sufficient sleep, Eat well-balanced food

DON'TS

Avoid lifting or carrying heavy weights, Stay away from heavyexercises on the dy of plasma donation, Avoid taking hot bath after plasma donation, Do not drink alcohol the day before or day of your donation, Avoid foods high in saturated fats and cholestrol,like hamburgers, potato chips and pizza the night before your donation
